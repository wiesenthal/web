Steve Wiesenthal provides comprehensive tax preparation, planning, consulting, accounting and business management to a wide range of individuals and businesses. He brings over 30 years of experience in the fields of accounting, tax compliance and planning.

Prior to opening his practice in Walnut Creek in 1990, Steve was a manager at Brown, Kraft & Co. of Los Angeles where he was responsible for the business, tax and financial management of businesses and individuals in a wide range of industries.

Upon college graduation, Steve joined PriceWaterhouseCoopers in New York where he was a senior tax consultant specializing in developing tax savings strategies for corporate executives, trusts and Fortune 500 companies.

Steve received his bachelor’s degree from Pace University in Business Administration. Steve is a Certified Public Accountant and a member of the California State Society of Certified Public Accountants.
